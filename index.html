What EchoxVIT do

Explore all the activities by our club.

01

StudX​

StudX empowers students to deliver seminars or ted style speech or presentations on impactful topics. These topics will cover areas that promote growth in both technical and non-technical fields. It’s a platform designed for students, by the students.​

02

WriteX​

EchoX gives students with valuable thoughts a platform to share their ideas through articles. These articles, covering both technical and non-technical themes, will be published on the club's website for a wider audience.​

03

ContentX​

EchoX VIT aims to equip students with essential digital content creation skills through its ContentX initiative

04

SpotlightX​

EchoxVIT empowers students through seminars by industry experts, focusing on primarily focuses on soft skills and personality development.​​
